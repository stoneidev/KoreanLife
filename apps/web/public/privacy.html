Privacy Policy for KoreanLife

Last updated: July 22, 2026

KoreanLife ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Progressive Web Application (PWA) operates and how we respect your personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

KoreanLife is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not require user account registration, user logins, or personal identification details (such as your name, email address, phone number, or exact geolocation).

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide a customized user experience, KoreanLife stores non-personal preferences locally on your browser/device (via LocalStorage):

  • Language preferences (English or Korean)
  • Saved/bookmarked guide items or offline data cache

This data remains strictly on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

3. Web Push Notifications

If you explicitly grant permission to receive push notifications, we store an anonymous Web Push subscription token to send periodic helpful tips and alerts regarding life in Korea. This token cannot be used to personally identify you or track your browsing activity.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process basic network logs (such as IP addresses and request headers) strictly for security and performance optimization purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us at: stoneidev@gmail.com
